Fintrack dashboard

A focused finance workspace for tracking investments, cashflow and budgets across multiple accounts — designed to make complex money decisions feel calm and obvious.

Visit live site Source
Client Fintrack Inc.
Role Lead designer · frontend
Timeline Jan – May 2025
Platform Web · iOS
Overview

From spreadsheets and
screenshots to one calm view.

Fintrack's customers were power users — accountants, indie investors, finance leads — running their lives on tabs and exports. The old dashboard worked, but it asked the user to do the synthesis. The brief: redesign the home view so the most important question, "am I okay?", is answerable in under five seconds.

01 · The challenge

Too many numbers, no narrative.

The legacy dashboard surfaced 38 metrics on one screen. Users could see everything but understand nothing — and bounce rates on the overview screen ran 47%.

02 · The approach

One headline, then context.

We led with a single answer — net worth, this week — then layered drilldowns. Hierarchy was rebuilt around 4 KPIs, one signature chart, and an allocation glance.

03 · The outcome

Decisions made in seconds.

Median time-to-insight dropped from 38s to 6s on first-week users. Retention at day 30 went from 41% to 62%. Mobile sessions tripled.

What we shipped

A workspace built on
one design principle:
answer first, detail second.

Every screen leads with the answer the customer came for, then unfolds context on demand. Drilldowns slide rather than navigate. Filters live where you'd reach for them.

  • Adaptive performance chart

    Toggle benchmarks, time windows and account groupings inline — no modal, no reload.

  • Live allocation donut

    Drag a slice to model rebalancing; the projected outcome updates in the chart in real time.

  • Unified transactions inbox

    All eight account types in one chronological stream, with smart category guesses you can correct in one keystroke.

  • Keyboard-first navigation

    Every action reachable in three keystrokes or fewer. Power users measured 4.2× faster task times.
